Why Schedule III is reuired as per the Companies Act

Effective April 1, 2021, the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) has implemented comprehensive disclosure requirements for financial statements, necessitating disclosure of transactions with companies that have been struck off by the Registrar of Companies (RoC) under section 248 of the Companies Act, 2013, or under section 560 of the Companies Act, 1956.

In such cases, the following details must be disclosed:

Name of the struck-off company
Nature of transactions with the company
Outstanding balance and relationship with the struck-off company
